The University of South Carolina is committed to providing an environment free from discrimination, harassment, and sexual misconduct, and related retaliation. This commitment helps realize the university’s
We ensure equity in all employment processes from time of hire to separation.
The purpose of this policy is to address consensual romantic and/or sexual relationships
between faculty, staff, students, and third-parties in which there is a power differential.

ADA Coordinator works closely with the Division of Human Resources and the Student
Disability Resource Center on reasonable accommodations for students, faculty, and
Discrimination, Harassment, and Sexual Misconduct Resolution Procedures
